# Paper Writing

## Purpose

Help AI agents write research papers and reports without fabricating sources or claims.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

Never fabricate:

- references
- citations
- DOI
- page numbers
- conference names
- journal names
- benchmark numbers
- ablation studies
- statistical significance

## Separate These Clearly

When writing about research, separate:

- claims made by the original paper
- our interpretation
- our implementation
- our experiment results
- limitations
- future work

## Evidence Rules

Do not say:

- state of the art
- significant improvement
- proven
- guaranteed

unless the evidence supports that exact claim.

## Citation Rules

If citation information is missing, write a placeholder and mark it clearly.

Do not invent BibTeX entries.

## Checklist

- [ ] No fabricated source
- [ ] No fabricated number
- [ ] Claims match evidence
- [ ] Limitations included
- [ ] Future work separated from result
